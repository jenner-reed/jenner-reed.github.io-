Meet Jenner

Jenner Reed writes the kind of romcoms that make you blush on public transit. Her stories feature bold heroines who say what they mean, heroes who need a little (okay, a lot of) emotional unpacking, and enough chemistry to set off a smoke detector.

When she's not writing, you can find her on bookstagram, overanalyzing fictional men, or losing arguments with her cats.
